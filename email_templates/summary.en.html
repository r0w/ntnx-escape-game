Nutanix Cloud Infrastructure (NCI) & Prism

Prism

Prism Central is the main interface when interacting with a Nutanix platform. It brings together most of the services hosted on our cloud platform and manages all or part of the Nutanix clusters you own.

Nutanix has done its utmost to make the Prism Central experience simple and intuitive, because we believe you don't need to be an expert to manage an IT platform day to day.

Multitenancy / RBAC

You experienced this through the use of projects and role assignments. The Nutanix platform enables a native multi-user approach by integrating with your directories and associating predefined or custom roles with fine-grained permissions. Each user only sees the assets they are entitled to access—just like services—helping prevent unauthorised interactions or disclosure across teams.

The power of categories

Nutanix provides categories in a key/value format that can be associated with most cluster elements (clusters, nodes, VMs, images, networks, etc.). Categories enable:

  • Quick identification of elements via searches using these categories
  • User rights management for categorised elements
  • Association with management policies (protection, security, storage, or affinity)

These categories can also be used by your backup tools to identify VMs to be protected.

Network management

Integrated IPAM

With AHV, Nutanix provides integrated IPAM. When you create a subnet, you can define an IP range that will be allocated to DHCP-configured VMs deployed in that network.

Software Defined Networking

Also with AHV, Nutanix offers a Software Defined Networking solution. Among other things, it enables you to:

  • Define micro-segmentation rules: filter network exchanges between your VMs and between your VMs and the outside world.
  • Define private networks that isolate your VMs from the rest of the cluster and allow IP reuse—for testing purposes, for example.

Integrated Disaster Recovery

Production needs protection, and backup is not always the best option for rapid recovery. Nutanix enables automated snapshots (with no storage overhead and minimal performance impact) and can replicate them to a second site if needed, with an RPO starting at 0. Failover can be orchestrated automatically (via a witness) or manually, letting you bring services back in the order that best fits operations.

Distributed, resilient, and secure storage

Nutanix pioneered hyperconvergence, which pools compute and storage resources into a single resource platform consumed by VMs. On the storage side, hyperconverged infrastructure must deliver resilience and security. Nutanix made technical choices to optimise distributed storage performance, reduce rebuild times in the event of disk or node failures, and natively manage data-reduction options (compression, deduplication, and/or erasure coding) as well as encryption (software or hardware).

Scaling

The Nutanix solution was built to deliver cloud-like flexibility, including the ability to scale without disruption. The goal is to avoid overbuying and overestimating your needs for the next five years. With Nutanix, you size for near-term requirements and expand later by adding nodes. Nutanix makes node expansion straightforward (as you saw), supports heterogeneous hardware, and helps maintain consistent performance as workloads grow.

Easy and safe upgrades

With LCM, Nutanix can update its software and firmware stack in just a few clicks. Forget hours spent comparing compatibility matrices and defining upgrade paths: Nutanix does it for you. In LCM, select the components proposed for upgrade, and the platform will check prerequisites and define the upgrade order. Come back a few hours later and your platform will be up to date—with no service interruption.

Nutanix Cloud Manager (NCM)

In addition to the core NCI component, Nutanix provides a set of capabilities designed to help customers keep their IT environments running smoothly. Here is an overview.

Customised dashboards and reports

The Prism Central interface can be customised to create dashboards tailored to each user's needs. These dashboards can be shared across teams for greater efficiency. Prism also enables fully customised reports that can be generated on demand or automatically, and optionally delivered to the recipients of your choice.

Sizing recommendations

Nutanix monitors your VMs daily and provides sizing recommendations. Too much CPU, not enough RAM, or a VM that does nothing—no need to hunt for it: you’ll get this information through alerts. Immediate benefits: resource savings and improved SLAs for your applications.

Smart Capacity Planning

Nutanix provides an intelligent capacity-planning engine that estimates when your cluster will reach capacity and can give you up to one year of visibility—helping you plan budgets in advance. Planning an acquisition or a new application rollout? Enter requirements, and capacity planning will recommend the most appropriate additions (node type, node count, and timing).

No-Code / Low-Code Automation

Everyone needs automation, but not everyone is a developer. Nutanix provides a low-code automation solution that lets you define a trigger and then execute actions of your choice—simple and effective. Example use cases:

  • Automatic VM categorisation
  • Automatic CMDB updates
  • Auto-remediation for incorrectly categorised VMs

Automation with a Marketplace

Nutanix provides its Self-Service solution for customers looking for more advanced automation. This multi-cloud automation engine lets you define and deploy blueprints through a marketplace, making the service catalog easily accessible to business teams. Deploy applications consistently on-prem and in the cloud, and implement approval workflows to control deployments.

Security dashboard

Nutanix detects asset vulnerabilities and compliance against current standards in a single dashboard. It’s not limited to data center assets: you can also integrate public cloud assets into the same tool to get an end-to-end view of your security posture.

What we didn’t cover today

NKP: Nutanix Kubernetes Platform

Nutanix offers a solution to deploy upstream Kubernetes clusters at scale across multiple clouds. Key features include:

  • Easy deployment, updates, and scaling of Kubernetes clusters.
  • Deployment of pre-configured and pre-validated application stacks to ensure interoperability across components.
  • Solutions to verify cluster health and enforce best practices for both clusters and applications.

NC2: Nutanix Cloud Cluster

Need to move to the cloud but worried about costs and lock-in? Nutanix lets you deploy Nutanix clusters with cloud operators and manage them the same way you manage your on-prem infrastructure, using the same interface.

  • No additional skills required.
  • Data center migrations are simplified and accelerated (simple VM migration).
  • No lock-in: you can change cloud providers by migrating assets from one NC2 cluster to another.

NUS: Nutanix Unified Storage

Nutanix provides several integrated storage services on its platform:

  • Block storage (iSCSI)
  • File storage (NFS and SMB/CIFS)
  • Object storage (S3)

All services can be deployed in a few clicks, helping you get value quickly with enterprise-grade features. You can also leverage Data Lens to map data, flag suspicious users, identify suspected ransomware activity, and more.

Nutanix Cost Governance

With NCM, Nutanix also provides a tool to manage costs: associate on-prem and cloud assets with cost centers or business units, track cost trends, monitor budget compliance, and apply savings recommendations. This helps you visualise and control your on-prem and cloud spending.
